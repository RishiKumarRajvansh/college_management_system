Allocation Guidelines

Please note the following guidelines for room allocation:

  • A student can only be allocated to one room at a time
  • Only available rooms are shown in the selection
  • Once allocated, the room status will be changed to 'occupied'
  • When a student vacates a room, the room status will be updated to 'available'
  • End date is optional but recommended for planning purposes
Important: Make sure to verify student information before allocation.
